About Us

Mulatiyana is one of the prominent education zones in the Southern province which locates in the Matara District in Sri Lanka. Mulatiyana zonal education office administers and operates the education quality development of all the educational institutions situated in the suburbs of Mulatiyana

Director’s Message

It’s my prior responsibility to make Mulatiyana Zonal
education office an effective institute which serve the
students, teachers, principals, non-academic staff members
and its locality by creating new avenues of education
through technology to suit the future world.
